Love learning. Hate school.​ Kids are bored sitting in classrooms taking tests about things that don’t always align with their interests or real life problems.​

inventorspaceXYZ is a nonprofit​ dedicated to bringing high-tech, hands-on education to all students regardless of income.​

We provide spaces and programs for kids to learn what they love in depth and apply it to the real world.​

inventorspaces

21st century spaces we set up in schools for kids to pursue STEM, Art, and Entrepreneurship.​

inventathons

24-hour hackathons for high and middle schoolers. Students form teams to build a new invention like a solar panel that moves with the sun a Virtual Reality game.

inventxcelerator

Students work with real Venture Capitalists and Angel Investors to convert the prototypes they make in an inventorspace or inventathon into real products.​
